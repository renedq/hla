Advances in Transplantation Immunology

Next Generation Sequencing, Epitope Analysis and MHC Antibodies in Organ and Stem Cell Transplantation.

Advances in gene sequencing and proteomics; Role of HLA in human disease: NGS typing of HLA and other MHC genes; Epitope analysis and matching; Measurement and interpretation of complex HLA antibodies; Clinical implication of new methods for transplantation matching and management; Introducing new technologies to the clinical laboratory; and Bioinformatics analysis of NGS and epitope data. Plenary speakers include: Dr. Henry Erlich, Roche Discovery Research and Human Genetics, Dr. Rene Duquesnoy, Professor Emeritus, University of Pittsburgh
